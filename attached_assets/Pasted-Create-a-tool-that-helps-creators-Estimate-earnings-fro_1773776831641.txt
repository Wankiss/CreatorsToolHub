⚙️ CALCULATION LOGIC
📌 Important Context

YouTube Shorts revenue comes from:

Ad revenue pool (shared across creators)

Not traditional CPM like long-form videos

Typical Shorts RPM range:

$0.01 – $0.10 per 1,000 views (can vary)

Step-by-Step Logic
1. Base RPM Range

Set base RPM depending on niche:

Entertainment/Comedy: $0.01 – $0.04

Gaming: $0.02 – $0.05

Lifestyle: $0.02 – $0.06

Education: $0.03 – $0.08

Tech/Finance: $0.04 – $0.10

2. Adjust by Location

Tier 1 (US, UK, Canada, Australia): +30–80%

Europe: +20–50%

Global: baseline

India/Nigeria: -20–50%

3. Adjust by Engagement Rate

High engagement (>10%): +30–70%

Medium (5–10%): baseline

Low (<5%): -20–40%

4. Partner Program Check

If NOT in YPP → earnings = $0

Show message: “You must be in the YouTube Partner Program to earn from Shorts ads”

5. Final RPM Calculation

final_rpm_low = base_low × multipliers
final_rpm_high = base_high × multipliers

6. Revenue Formula

estimated_low = (views / 1000) × final_rpm_low
estimated_high = (views / 1000) × final_rpm_high

average = (low + high) / 2

7. Monthly / Yearly Projection

If monthly views provided:

monthly_revenue = calculation

yearly = monthly × 12
