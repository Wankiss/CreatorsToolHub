1. CONTENT TYPE DISTRIBUTION ENGINE

1 Carousel

Example:

Education

Entertainment

Personal

Promotion

- Ensure each pillar appears weekly
- Avoid repeating same pillar back-to-back
- Rotate evenly across the week

Type	Purpose
Reels	Reach, discovery
Carousel	Saves, education
Static	Authority, branding
Stories	Engagement, trust
4. TOPIC GENERATION ENGINE
Each topic must be:

Specific (not vague)

Relevant to audience pain points

Aligned with pillar

✅ “3 mistakes stopping your fat loss”

Each post includes a content angle:

- Curiosity-based
- Contrarian
- Story-driven
- Quick-win
6. GOAL ASSIGNMENT
Each post must have a goal:

Reach (Reels)

Saves (Carousels)

Comments (engagement posts)

Sales (offers)

Morning: 7–9 AM
