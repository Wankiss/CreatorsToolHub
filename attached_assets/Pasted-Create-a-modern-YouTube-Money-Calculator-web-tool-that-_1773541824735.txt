Core Calculator Logic

YouTube earnings are typically calculated using RPM (Revenue per 1000 views) rather than CPM.

Formula:

Estimated Earnings = (Views / 1000) × RPM

Where:

RPM = Monetized CPM × Monetized Play Rate

Realistic YouTube Metrics

Use these realistic industry averages:

Monetized Play Rate
= 40% – 70%

Ad CPM (Advertiser CPM)
= $1 – $30 depending on niche

YouTube takes about 45% revenue share.

So:

Creator RPM ≈ CPM × 0.55 × Monetized Play Rate

Example:

CPM = $10
Monetized rate = 50%

RPM = 10 × 0.55 × 0.5
RPM = $2.75

Calculation Flow

Step 1

Get CPM range from niche table

Example
Tech = $10 – $25

Step 2

Apply geographic multiplier

Example
Tier 4 (Africa) multiplier = 0.25

Adjusted CPM:

Low = 10 × 0.25 = 2.5
High = 25 × 0.25 = 6.25

Step 3

Convert CPM to RPM

RPM = CPM × 0.55 × Monetized Rate

Example:

CPM = 2.5
Monetized Rate = 50%

RPM = 2.5 × 0.55 × 0.5
RPM = $0.69

Step 4

Calculate earnings

Earnings = (Views / 1000) × RPM
