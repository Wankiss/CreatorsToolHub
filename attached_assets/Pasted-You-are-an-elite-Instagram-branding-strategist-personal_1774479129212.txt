⚙️ CORE BIO GENERATION LOGIC
This is what ensures bios are clear, punchy, and conversion-focused 👇

1. BIO STRUCTURE ENGINE
Each bio should follow this formula:

[Who you help] + [What result you deliver] + [How/angle] + [CTA]
🔹 Example:
“Helping busy moms lose weight without strict diets | Follow for daily tips”

2. CHARACTER LIMIT CONTROL
🔹 Hard Rule:
Max = 150 characters (strict)
Ideal = 90–140 characters
🔹 AI must:
Count characters before output

Trim unnecessary words

Replace long phrases with shorter alternatives

3. TONE ENGINE
🔹 PROFESSIONAL (3 outputs)
Clear and authoritative

Focus on results and expertise

Slightly formal

Example style:

“Fitness coach helping professionals lose fat sustainably | Follow for proven strategies”

🔹 BOLD (2 outputs)
Confident, direct

Slightly edgy or contrarian

Example style:

“Your diet isn’t broken—you are | Fix it here ↓”

🔹 MINIMAL (2 outputs)
Clean, short

Straight to the point

Example style:

“Fat loss made simple | Daily tips ↓”

🔹 INSPIRATIONAL (3 outputs)
Motivational, emotional

Future-focused

Example style:

“Become the version of you you respect | Start today ↓”

4. VALUE CLARITY LOGIC
Each bio must answer instantly:

Who is this for?

What do I gain by following?

🔹 Weak:
❌ “Helping you grow”

🔹 Strong:
✅ “Helping creators grow to 10K followers”

5. CTA ENGINE
🔹 CTA TYPES:
Type	Example
Follow	“Follow for daily tips”
DM	“DM ‘START’ to begin”
Link	“Get the guide ↓”
🔹 Rules:
- Max 1 CTA
- Must feel natural
- Placed at end
6. CREDIBILITY BOOSTER (OPTIONAL)
If user provides proof:

Add:

Numbers

Results

Achievements

🔹 Example:
“Helped 500+ clients lose weight”

7. FORMATTING LOGIC
🔹 Readability styles:
Single line

Split with “|”

Line breaks

🔹 Example:
Helping creators grow on IG  
10K+ in 90 days  
Follow for daily tips ↓
8. PERSONALIZATION ENGINE
🔹 Adapt based on:
Audience sophistication

Niche competitiveness

Tone preference

9. QUALITY CONTROL FILTER
Before output:

Ensure under 150 characters

Remove fluff words

Ensure CTA exists

Ensure niche is clear

Ensure value is specific
