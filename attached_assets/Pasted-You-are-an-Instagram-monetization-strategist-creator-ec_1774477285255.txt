2. REVENUE STREAM FORMULAS

Estimated Reach per Post = Followers × 30% (Reels bias)

CPM = $5–$25

Price per Post =
(Reach / 1000) × CPM × Engagement Multiplier × Niche Multiplier
Monthly:

Posts per month × Price per post
🔗 B. AFFILIATE MARKETING
Clicks = Followers × 2% × Engagement Multiplier

Conversions = Clicks × (0.5%–3%)

Revenue =
Conversions × Avg Product Price × Commission Rate (5–30%)
💎 C. INSTAGRAM SUBSCRIPTIONS
Subscribers =
Followers × (0.5%–5%)

Revenue =
Subscribers × Subscription Price ($2.99–$9.99)
🛍️ D. PRODUCT SALES
Buyers =
Followers × (0.5%–2.5%)

Revenue =
Buyers × Product Price
