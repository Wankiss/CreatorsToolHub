Hashtag Generation Logic

When user clicks Generate Hashtags, follow this logic.

Step 1 — Extract Keywords from Topic

Example topic:

How to grow on YouTube in 2026


Extract keywords:

YouTube
YouTube growth
YouTube SEO
content creation
YouTube tips

Step 2 — Generate Hashtag Variations

Convert keywords to hashtags.

Rules:

remove spaces

capitalize words

keep readable

Example:

#YouTubeGrowth
#YouTubeSEO
#ContentCreator
#YouTubeTips

Step 3 — Generate Long-Tail Hashtags

Combine keywords with modifiers.

Examples:

#GrowOnYouTube
#YouTubeGrowthTips
#YouTubeAlgorithm
#YouTubeCreatorTips


Purpose:

Long-tail hashtags improve niche discoverability on YouTube.

Step 4 — Generate Trending Hashtags

Include widely used platform hashtags.

Examples:

#YouTube
#ContentCreator
#VideoMarketing
#YouTuber
#CreatorEconomy


Rules:

Use only relevant hashtags.

Avoid spam hashtags like:

#viral
#fyp
#like4like

Step 5 — Categorize Hashtags

Group hashtags into categories.

1 Trending Hashtags

High-volume hashtags.

Example:

#YouTube
#YouTuber
#ContentCreator

2 Niche Hashtags

Specific to the video topic.

Example:

#YouTubeSEO
#GrowOnYouTube
#YouTubeTips

3 Long-Tail Hashtags

More specific.

Example:

#YouTubeGrowthStrategy
#YouTubeSEO2026
#GrowYourChannel

Step 6 — Limit Hashtags

YouTube best practice:

3–15 hashtags recommended


However the generator can produce 20 suggestions so users can choose.

    topic = getTopic()
    niche = getNiche()
    keywords = getKeywords()

    extractedKeywords = extractKeywords(topic)

    hashtags = []

    for keyword in extractedKeywords
        hashtags.add(convertToHashtag(keyword))

    addTrendingHashtags(niche)

    addLongTailHashtags(keywords)

    removeDuplicates()

    limitToUserQuantity()

    displayHashtags()
