⚙️ KEYWORD GENERATION LOGIC
1. Keyword Expansion

Start with seed keyword and generate variations:

Add modifiers:

“how to”

“best”

“easy”

“for beginners”

“2025”

“step by step”

Create long-tail variations:

“[keyword] for beginners”

“[keyword] without [pain point]”

2. Search Volume Estimation

Use heuristic scoring:

Base volume score (1–100)

Adjust by:

Keyword length (shorter = higher volume)

Trend relevance

Niche popularity

3. Competition Estimation

Simulate competition score based on:

Broad keywords → High competition

Long-tail keywords → Low competition

4. Opportunity Score

Formula:

opportunity_score = (search_volume × 0.6) + (low_competition × 0.4)

Highlight keywords with:

High volume + low competition
